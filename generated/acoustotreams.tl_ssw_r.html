acoustotreams.tl_ssw_r¶

acoustotreams.tl_ssw_r(lambda_, mu, l, m, kr, theta, phi)¶

Regular translation coefficient of scalar and longitudinal vector spherical waves

Definded by

\[\begin{split}\alpha_{\lambda \mu l m}^{(1)}(x, \theta, \varphi) = (-1)^m \mathrm{i}^{\lambda-l} \sqrt{4\pi (2l+1)(2\lambda+1)} \\ \cdot \sum_q \mathrm{i}^{q} \sqrt{2q+1} j_q(x) Y_{q,m-\mu}(\theta,\varphi) \\ \cdot \begin{pmatrix} l& \lambda & q \\ m & -\mu & \mu-m \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} l& \lambda & q \\ 0& 0& 0 \end{pmatrix}\end{split}\]

with the Wigner 3j-symbols (acoustotreams.wigner3j()), the spherical Bessel functions (acoustotreams.spherical_jn()), and the spherical harmonics (acoustotreams.sph_harm()). The summation runs over all \(q \in \{\lambda + l, \lambda + l - 2, \dots, \max(|\lambda - l|, |\mu - m|)\}\).

These coefficients are used to translate from scattered to scattered modes and from incident to incident modes.

Parameters:

  • lambda (integer, array_like) – Degree of the destination mode

  • mu (integer, array_like) – Order of the destination mode

  • l (integer, array_like) – Degree of the source mode

  • m (integer, array_like) – Order of the source mode

  • x (complex, array_like) – Translation in units of the wave number in the medium

  • theta (float or complex, array_like) – Polar angle

  • phi (float, array_like) – Azimuthal angel

Returns:

complex
