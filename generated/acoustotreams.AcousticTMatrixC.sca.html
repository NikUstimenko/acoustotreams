acoustotreams.AcousticTMatrixC.sca¶

AcousticTMatrixC.sca(inc)[source]¶

Expansion coefficients of the scattered field.

Possible for all T-matrices (global and local) in non-absorbing embedding. The coefficients are calculated by

\[p_{k_z m} = T_{k_z m,{k_z}'m'} a_{{k_z}'m'}\]

where \(a_{k_z m}\) are the expansion coefficients of the incident wave, \(T\) is the T-matrix.

Parameters:

inc (complex, array) – Incident wave or its expansion coefficients

Returns:

AcousticsArray
