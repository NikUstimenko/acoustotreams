acoustotreams.scw_rPsi¶

acoustotreams.scw_rPsi(kz, m, krr, phi, z)[source]¶

Regular scalar cylindrical wave \(\Psi\)

The regular scalar cylindrical wave is defined by

\[ \psi_{k_z m}^{(1)}(x_\rho, \varphi, z) = J_m(x_\rho) \mathrm e^{\mathrm i (k_z z + m \varphi)}\]

using acoustotreams.jv().

This function describes a solution to the scalar Helmholtz wave equation.

Parameters:

  • kz (float, array_like) – Z component of the wave vector

  • m (int, array_like) – Order \(|m| \leq l\)

  • xrho (float or complex, array_like) – Radial distance in units of the radial component of the wave number in the medium \(k_\rho \rho\)

  • phi (float, array_like) – Azimuthal angle

  • z (float, array_like) – Z coordinate

Returns:

complex
